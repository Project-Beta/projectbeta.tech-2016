About Us



—  ProjectBeta is Sanskriti School's prestigious tech club. Our society is headed by President Akshat Pratap Singh and Vice President Sammad Grover, both of whom are extremely passionate about technology. Our club consists of 30+ members who have specialised in various fields such as Programming, Web Designing, App Development, Audio & Video editing, Digital Imaging, 3D Designing, Animation among others.

Tournament



—  Founded in 2013, ProjectBeta is Sanskriti School's IT Club. After 3 years spent in emergence as an indispensable component of the Sanskriti fraternity, the club is proud to host the first iteration of its eponymous inter-school IT tournament, ProjectBeta 2016.

—  We will be hosting an array of events; Programming Championship, Gaming Championship, an Audio/Visual Tournament, an IT Quiz, Group Discussion and a Classified event.

—  The event will be taking place on the 1st of October. You can find the registration form here. We hope to see you soon.
